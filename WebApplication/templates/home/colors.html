CBAM(Convolutional Block Attention Module) :
It adds spatial and channel-wise attention, improving the model focus on important features while ignoring noise.
CBAM is applied after each upsample and fusion step, improving the representation before passing it further.
Channel Attention
– Focuses on the important parts(What)
Spatial Attention
– Focuses on the important sides(Where)
